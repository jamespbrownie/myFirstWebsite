About me:
Born in Manhattan, grew up in Mount Vernon, NY. Went to catholic highschool, ran track there. Went to Skidmore College in Saratoga Springs NY. My love of bicycles and the outdoors grew there. Got a compost-bicycle job (???) in Austin, TX so I moved there after graduation. Spent 6 years riding bikes and vibing in Austin, made a lot of good friends and memories along the way, and then realized I actually need to learn a marketable skill. Enrolled in the Flatiron School, and here I am!
